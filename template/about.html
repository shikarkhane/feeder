About us

define tip-off: a piece of information, typically one given in a discreet or confidential way.

tipoff is an effort to help you know your neighbourhood better.

We spend 80% of our lives living in a 5km radius. And with the amazing social media, we should be more congizant of our neighbourhood vibes.

Also its more fun to know when your favourite bakery is baking something special with a good offer.

tipoff users see all geo-tagged content being posted around them. To begin with, we are featuring twitter content. When you embedd a web url link in a tweet, it becomes clickable on our site.

We will integrate with more and more geo-tagged content providers like Facebook, Instagram etc while keeping it clutter-free for our users. In general, we delete a tipoff and related content after 3 days. The content retention period is subjected to vary in future.

You can contact us at contact@tipoff.io
